### MC
- 40 files of ~19k events each (PYTHIA 6.1 through full ALEPH detector simulation)
- Total: 771,597 events (both reco and gen-level available)
- Generator-level tree `tgen` includes truth particles with selection flags
- `tgenBefore` tree: gen-level before event selection (24,360 per file vs 19,158 after)

### Key branches
- Particle-level: px, py, pz, pt, pmag, eta, theta, phi, mass, charge, pwflag, pid, d0, z0
- Event-level: Thrust (+ TTheta, TPhi), Sphericity, Aplanarity, nParticle, Energy
- Selection flags: passesAll, passesNTrkMin, passesSTheta, passesMissP, etc.
- pwflag: 0=charged tracks, 4=photons, 5=neutral hadrons
